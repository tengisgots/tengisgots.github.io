What is HxH?

HxH aka Hunter x Hunter is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi. It also happens to be my most favorite anime out of the 260+ series I have seen! Now let me walk you through the basic plotline and why it's so interesting so maybe you can take an interest in HxH too!

Gon Freecss

Above shown in the image is Gon Freecss the main character of the show HxH. Although he's the main character the story has several central characters which makes the story not only focused on one main character but several, which shows the story develop from multiple points of views! In fact in the latest few chapters of the manga Gon does not appear at all instead focusing on Kurapika, another central character.

The story for most of the first part focuses on the friendship and adventures of Gon and Killua (The one with the silver hair) which is Gon's best friend throughout the story. In the story Gon is a sheltered innocent kid while Killua is a trained assassin for childhood often shown to be ruthless and having no regard for human life. But as the story develops Killua starts developing a more empathetic side while Gon turns ruthless due to many traumatic experiences which shows the stark difference in character development!

Out of all these amazing and well-developed character my favorite happens to be Kurapika! Kurapika is the last survivor of the Kurta Clan, whose scarlet eyes were stolen by the Phantom Troupe( Also known as the Spiders, shown in the image) after they massacred his people, leading him to become a Hunter and seek revenge by targeting the Troupe and recovering the stolen eyes..
